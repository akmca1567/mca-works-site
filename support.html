Support

Thanks for using our apps. We're a small team based in Anchorage, Alaska, and we read every message.

Contact: support@mca-works.com
We aim to respond within 3 business days.

Reporting a bug

To help us resolve issues quickly, please include the following in your message:

  • The app name and version (found in the app's settings)
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • A description of what you were doing when the issue occurred
  • Screenshots if relevant

Feature requests

We welcome suggestions. Send your ideas to the same address above. We can't promise we'll build everything, but every request is read and considered.

Refunds and billing

App Store purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple. To request a refund, please use Apple's refund process at reportaproblem.apple.com. We are unable to issue refunds directly.

Account and data

Our apps do not use user accounts. Your data is stored on your device and, when enabled, synchronized through your private iCloud container. To remove all data, delete the app from your device; this also removes any associated iCloud data after a short period.

Privacy

For information on how our apps handle data, see our Privacy Policy.
